About Me

LIFE SO FAR

I was born, raised, and am living in Miami-Dade County, Florida. I consider myself a multi-talented human. Most of my work is designing and building in the digital world. I am very much interested and am passionate about the interconnectedness of UI & UX design--making products look sleek and pleasurable with a relevant and meaningful experience.

In this computerized world we are living in, things are continuously advancing and changing, which in turn motivates me to learn all the exciting things the landscape has to offer. Some would say that I am a fast learner, and I would attribute that to my fascination with absorbing new skills and information. As a perpetual optimist, I feel that I can juggle an assortment of areas of study and artistic ventures with relative ease.

As for the analog world, I figure myself to be a "Culturista" (i.e. pop culture aficionado). I enjoy movies, musicals, anime, and music, especially live (not in that order). Beer and cosplay are among my passions as well. Feel free to drop me a line, if you'd like.
